About Us
Solomon Restaurant was founded in 2021 out of passion for amazing food and and service.The then small operation saw a rapid growth after it got its very first big contract to serve hundreds of customers in a day.Through a lot of hard work and commitment, we found a large restaurant with 500 capacity(people).Solomon restaurant has become one of the most loved and visited restaurant in Nigeria.

We believe that using the freshest ingredients from nature brings out the best results.We are tireless in staying abreast of the latest food trends,and we are obsessed with creating customized menus and culinary masterpieces for our customers.
